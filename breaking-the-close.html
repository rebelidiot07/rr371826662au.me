Breaking the Close — No Trespass

i say, this notice concerns this threshold and the place immediately connected with it only;

i say, no claim is made by i over common land, common access, shared pathways, stairs, driveways, letterboxes, or any part not given to i to control;

i say, paper may be left only at the paper point made for paper;

i say, no consent is implied by postal habit, routing instruction, mailbox, path, stair, landing, gate, door, bell, number, sign, custom, usage, paper, record, office, badge, title, or name;

i say, no man has consent from i to enter, knock, inspect, search, photograph, interfere with, disturb, or remain at this threshold;

i say, leaving paper at the paper point does not create consent, joinder, appearance, surety, agreement, liability, contract, service admission, or acceptance of any name, person, address, account, claim, office, or title;

i say, any man claiming authority to proceed beyond the paper point is asked to identify the man on whose first-hand authority he acts;

i ask, what man enters, and by what authority?
